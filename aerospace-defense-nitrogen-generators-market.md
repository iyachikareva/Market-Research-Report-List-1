<p><ul><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Cryogenic Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aerospace & Defense Nitrogen Generators market includes three main types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air systems. PSA utilizes adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from other gases at varying pressures, making it efficient for high purity needs. Membrane technology uses selective permeation through hollow fibers to separate nitrogen, ideal for lower volume applications. Cryogenic air separation liquefies air, then distills it to produce nitrogen, offering high volume and purity, suitable for large-scale aerospace and defense operations.</p></p>
