<p><ul><li>Acrylic Cast Plate</li><li>Acrylic Extrusion Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace grade acrylic market primarily consists of two types: acrylic cast plates and acrylic extrusion sheets. Acrylic cast plates are manufactured through a casting process, resulting in a thicker, more robust product with superior optical clarity, making them suitable for applications like cockpit canopies. In contrast, acrylic extrusion sheets are produced by forcing acrylic through a mold, allowing for thinner and customizable dimensions, often used in interior components and lightweight structures. Both types serve critical roles in aerospace design and manufacturing.</p></p>
