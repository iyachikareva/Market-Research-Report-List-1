<p><p>The baby car seat market includes three primary types: infant car seats, convertible car seats, and booster seats. Infant car seats are rear-facing, designed for newborns and smaller infants, ensuring maximum safety. Convertible car seats can transition from rear-facing to forward-facing, accommodating children as they grow. Booster seats elevate older children to safely use standard seat belts. Each type serves distinct age and weight requirements, prioritizing child safety during travel in vehicles while adapting to their developmental stages.</p></p>
