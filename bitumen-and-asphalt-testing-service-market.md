<p><p>The Bitumen and Asphalt Testing Service market encompasses various testing types essential for evaluating the performance and quality of asphalt materials. Ductility Testing measures the elongation of bitumen under stress, indicating its flexibility. Penetration Testing assesses the hardness or softness of bitumen, influencing its suitability for different climates. Emulsion Testing evaluates stability and residue characteristics, while Relative Density Testing determines the density of asphalt mixtures. Additional tests may include viscosity and thermal stability analyses, ensuring optimal road construction and maintenance.</p></p>
