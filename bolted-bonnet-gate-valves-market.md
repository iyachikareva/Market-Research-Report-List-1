<p><p>The bolted bonnet gate valves market consists of two primary connection types: socket weld connections and flanged connections. Socket weld connections offer a seamless and compact fitting ideal for high-pressure applications, ensuring a strong joint that minimizes the risk of leakage. In contrast, flanged connections provide ease of installation and maintenance, making them suitable for larger pipelines and systems where rapid disassembly is necessary. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring reliable fluid control in various operating conditions.</p></p>
