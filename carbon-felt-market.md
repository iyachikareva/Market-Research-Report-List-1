Rigid felt, on the other hand, offers structural support and enhanced thermal resistance, typically used in high-temperature applications like aerospace and industrial furnaces. Both types leverage carbon's unique properties to enhance performance in various industries, including energy, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
