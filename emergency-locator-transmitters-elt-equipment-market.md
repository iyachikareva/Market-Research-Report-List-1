<p><p>Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs) are essential safety devices used in aviation and maritime industries to enhance search and rescue operations. The market includes two primary types: fixed ELTs and portable ELTs. Fixed ELTs are permanently installed in aircraft and activate automatically upon impact, transmitting distress signals to facilitate location by rescuers. In contrast, portable ELTs are handheld devices that can be activated manually and are typically used by individuals or in smaller vessels, providing flexibility for various emergency scenarios.</p></p>
