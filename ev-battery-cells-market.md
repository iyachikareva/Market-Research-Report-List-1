<p><p>The EV battery cells market primarily comprises three types: Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) battery cells, Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) battery cells, and Other battery cells. Li-ion cells dominate due to their high energy density, lightweight nature, and efficiency, making them ideal for electric vehicles. NiMH cells are favored in hybrid vehicles for their reliability and longer life cycle, though they have lower energy density. Other battery technologies, such as solid-state and sodium-ion, are emerging, aiming for improved safety and sustainability.</p></p>
