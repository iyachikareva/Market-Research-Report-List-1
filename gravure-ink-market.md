Water type gravure inks are environmentally friendly, ideal for packaging due to their quick drying properties. Alcoholic type gravure inks offer fast evaporation and excellent adhesion, commonly used in flexible packaging. Benzene type gravure inks provide vibrant colors and are favored for high-quality prints, although less common due to safety concerns. Petrol type gravure inks are versatile and suitable for various substrates, often used in industrial applications.</p></p>
