<p><p>The heart defects closure device market includes various specialized devices designed to treat specific cardiac anomalies. Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) closure devices are used to close holes between the heart's upper chambers. Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) closure devices aim to reduce stroke risk by sealing off the LAA. Patent Foramen Ovale (PFO) closure devices address abnormal openings between the atria. Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA) closure devices close passages between the aorta and pulmonary artery, while Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) closure devices target holes between the heart's lower chambers. Other devices may address additional heart anomalies.</p></p>
