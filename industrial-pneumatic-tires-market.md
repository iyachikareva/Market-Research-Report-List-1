<p><p>The industrial pneumatic tire market is primarily divided into two types: solid pneumatic tires and air-filled pneumatic tires. Solid pneumatic tires are made from rubber compounds and provide durability and puncture resistance, making them ideal for demanding environments. On the other hand, air-filled pneumatic tires feature an inner air chamber that offers excellent shock absorption and traction, suitable for various terrain. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like construction, warehousing, and logistics, enhancing operational efficiency and equipment performance.</p></p>
