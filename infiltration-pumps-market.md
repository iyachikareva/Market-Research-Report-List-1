<p><p>Infiltration pumps are specialized devices used for groundwater management and environmental monitoring. The market for these pumps includes two main types: non-conductive roller head and conductive roller head. Non-conductive roller heads are designed to prevent electrical interference, making them suitable for environments with sensitive electronic equipment. In contrast, conductive roller heads facilitate electrical conductivity, making them ideal for applications where monitoring of electrically charged fluids is essential. Both types cater to specific needs in various industries, including environmental engineering and water resource management.</p></p>
