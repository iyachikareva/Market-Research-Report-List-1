<p><p>The laminated glass interlayer film market primarily consists of two main types: EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) interlayer film and PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) interlayer film. EVA film is known for its superior clarity, flexibility, and resistance to UV light, making it ideal for applications requiring enhanced aesthetics and durability. On the other hand, PVB film is widely used for its strong adhesion and safety benefits, commonly found in automotive and architectural glass, providing impact resistance and sound insulation. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing glass performance and safety.</p></p>
