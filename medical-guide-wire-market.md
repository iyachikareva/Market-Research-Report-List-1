<p><p>The medical guide wire market is segmented into three primary types: straight, angled, and J-shape guide wires. Straight medical guide wires are used for straightforward access during procedures, offering direct navigation through vasculature. Angled guide wires provide enhanced maneuverability in curved anatomical pathways, facilitating navigation in complex regions. J-shape guide wires feature a distinctive curved end, ideal for guiding devices around tight turns. Each type is essential in various interventional procedures, ensuring precise delivery and placement of medical devices.</p></p>
