<p><strong>The Metallic Spray Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic spray paint market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic spray paints contain organic solvents, offering excellent durability, adhesion, and a smooth finish, making them ideal for industrial applications. However, they may emit strong fumes. In contrast, water-based metallic spray paints utilize water as a solvent, providing lower volatility and easier cleanup. They are more environmentally friendly and less hazardous, making them suitable for residential projects but may require multiple coats for full coverage.</p></p>
