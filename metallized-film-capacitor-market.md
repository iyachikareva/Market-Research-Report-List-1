<p><p>Metallized film capacitors are passive electronic components that utilize a thin film of metal as the electrode. <p><p>The metallized film capacitor market is divided into AC and DC segments based on their application in electrical systems. AC metallized film capacitors are used in alternating current applications, beneficial for filtering, power factor correction, and energy storage due to their high-voltage tolerance and stability. In contrast, DC metallized film capacitors are designed for direct current applications, offering low leakage current and high insulation resistance, making them ideal for power electronics, energy conversion, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
