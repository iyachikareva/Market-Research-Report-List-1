<p><p>The Natural Diatomite market is categorized into two primary types: Anhydrous Substance and Flux Calcined. Anhydrous Substance diatomite consists of naturally occurring diatomaceous earth that is not altered by heat, retaining its original properties and structure. In contrast, Flux Calcined diatomite undergoes a thermal treatment that enhances its reactivity and porosity, making it suitable for various industrial applications, such as filtration, insulation, and as a filler in products. Both types serve distinct functions across multiple sectors.</p></p>
