<p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) systems market is categorized into three main types: TD-OCT (Time-Domain OCT), SD-OCT (Spectral-Domain OCT), and SS-OCT (Swept Source OCT). TD-OCT offers lower resolution and speed, primarily used for basic imaging. SD-OCT provides improved resolution and faster imaging times, making it suitable for detailed assessments in ophthalmology. SS-OCT, the most advanced, enables deeper tissue penetration and faster data acquisition, ideal for a range of clinical applications, particularly in complex anatomical regions.</p></p>
