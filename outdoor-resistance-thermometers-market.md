<p><p>The outdoor resistance thermometer market primarily features two types: threaded and flanged resistance thermometers. Threaded resistance thermometers are designed for direct insertion into measuring points, ensuring effective thermal contact for accurate temperature readings. They are commonly used in pipelines and tanks. Flanged resistance thermometers, on the other hand, are integrated into flanged connections, providing robust installation options for larger equipment and industrial applications. Both types are essential for reliable temperature monitoring in various outdoor environments, ensuring operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
