<p><p>The Oxidation Reduction Potential (ORP) electrodes market is primarily categorized into single junction and double junction types. Single junction ORP electrodes feature a simple design with a single electrode body, providing direct measurement of redox potential but are more susceptible to contamination. In contrast, double junction ORP electrodes include an additional reference junction, enhancing stability and reducing the risk of contamination, making them ideal for applications in challenging environments. Both types are essential for various industrial and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
