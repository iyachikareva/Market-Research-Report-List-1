<p><p>Polycarbonate thermoplastic (PCTA) is categorized into three main market types: Injection Grade, Extruded Grade, and Blow Molding Grade. Injection Grade PCTA is used for producing intricate shapes and components through injection molding, offering high strength and clarity. Extruded Grade is ideal for products requiring continuous lengths, like sheets and films, providing excellent dimensional stability and surface finish. Blow Molding Grade is suitable for making hollow objects, such as bottles and containers, by allowing easy mold filling and shaping, ensuring durability and lightweight characteristics.</p></p>
