<p><p>The PE container liner market primarily consists of two key types: PP container liners and PE container liners. PP (polypropylene) container liners are known for their excellent durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for transporting granular materials. On the other hand, PE (polyethylene) container liners are favored for their flexibility and moisture barrier properties, making them suitable for bulk liquids and sensitive products. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring safe and efficient transportation and storage of various goods.</p></p>
