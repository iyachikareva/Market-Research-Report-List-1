<p><p>Printed electronics encompass various manufacturing techniques that facilitate the production of electronic components using printing methods. Inkjet printing allows precise deposition of materials, ideal for intricate designs. Gravure printing offers high-speed production with excellent uniformity, suitable for large-area applications. Screen printing enables thick layers of materials, favored for durability. Flexography provides versatility and efficiency for large runs on flexible substrates. Other methods include offset and 3D printing, further expanding the capabilities of printed electronics in diverse applications.</p></p>
