<p><ul><li>Active RFID Tag</li><li>Passive RFID Tag</li><li>Semi-active RFID Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Retail RFID tag market encompasses three main types: Active RFID tags, Passive RFID tags, and Semi-active RFID tags. Active RFID tags have their own power source, enabling long-range communications and advanced functionalities. Passive RFID tags, lacking a power source, rely on energy from the reader and are cost-effective for high-volume use, like inventory tracking. Semi-active RFID tags combine features of both, using a battery for additional power but still relying on external readers for communication, making them versatile for various retail applications.</p></p>
