<p><p>Slip-resistant flooring is categorized based on the R-rating system, which measures the traction provided under different conditions. R6-R8 ratings indicate low slip resistance, suitable for areas with minimal moisture. R9 offers moderate resistance, appropriate for dry indoor spaces. R10 ratings provide good traction for bathrooms and kitchens, while R11 ratings are designed for high-risk environments like commercial kitchens, ensuring safety in wet or oily conditions. These ratings help consumers choose appropriate flooring for specific applications based on safety needs.</p></p>
