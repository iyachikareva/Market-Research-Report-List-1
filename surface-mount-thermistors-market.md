<p><p>Surface mount thermistors are classified into several types based on their form factor and configuration. End-banded chip thermistors feature conductive pads on the ends for soldering, enabling compact placement on PCBs. MELF (Metal Electrode Leadless Face) style thermistors have a cylindrical shape, offering robust mounting and stable performance. Top/bottom terminated chip thermistors have contact pads on both sides, facilitating versatile mounting options. Each type serves specific applications in temperature sensing and control within electronic devices, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
