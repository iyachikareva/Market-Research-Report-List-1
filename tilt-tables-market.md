<p><p>Tilt tables are categorized into two primary market types: electric and manual. Electric tilt tables are powered by motors, allowing for smooth and adjustable positioning with minimal effort, often used in clinical settings for rehabilitation. Manual tilt tables rely on physical force for adjustments, providing hands-on control but requiring more effort from caregivers. Both types serve essential roles in patient therapy, helping individuals improve their balance, mobility, and overall physical function. Each type caters to different needs and environments.</p></p>
