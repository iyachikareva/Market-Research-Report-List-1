<p><p>The turbidity sensors market is categorized into two main types: analog turbidity sensors and digital turbidity sensors. Analog turbidity sensors provide continuous voltage output proportional to the turbidity level, making them suitable for real-time monitoring in various applications. In contrast, digital turbidity sensors utilize advanced signal processing to convert measurements into digital formats, enabling higher accuracy and integration with modern data systems. Both types are crucial for water quality assessments across industries like environmental monitoring, aquaculture, and wastewater management.</p></p>
