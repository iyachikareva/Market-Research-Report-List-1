<p><p>The Typhoid Fever Vaccines market comprises several types. Live attenuated vaccines use weakened bacteria to stimulate immunity effectively. Conjugate vaccines combine polysaccharides from the bacteria's surface with proteins to enhance immune response, offering longer-lasting protection. Capsular polysaccharide vaccines utilize purified capsular material from the bacteria to induce immunity. Other vaccine types may include inactivated vaccines or novel formulations. Each type aims to provide varying degrees of protection, safety profiles, and suitability for different age groups and populations.</p></p>
