<p><p>The vestibular testing system market includes several types, primarily videonystagmography (VNG) and electronystagmography (ENG). VNG utilizes video cameras to record eye movements and assess vestibular function, providing real-time analysis of balance disorders. ENG, on the other hand, measures electrical activity in the eyes, helping to detect abnormalities in the vestibular system. Other testing modalities may include rotary chair tests and posturography, each contributing to a comprehensive understanding of balance issues and guiding appropriate treatment strategies.</p></p>
